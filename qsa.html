How difficult it is to stay programmer?

It's pretty personal thing - depends on you and area you plan to work with. If you choose front-end development in web you wouldn't need that much time to start working that you will spend on learning how to make high quality programs on C++

How much time I need to spend on studying to stay junior?

The same as in previous answer, it's very personal. But usually it takes more than half a year even for the easiest areas (if you start from 0).

How to choose what the area is the best to start with?

Nowadays all programs, companies and even some games have it's web version, so web development is one of the most in-demand areas. At the same time, you can start way easier than in other areas, becouse there's no need in deep knowledge of math, phisics, languages etc.

how much a day is enough to study programming?

As more as possible. As faster you want to stay at least junior developer as more time you need to spend. But if we talk about minimal time, I'd say that if you study programming even less than 1-2 hours a day it won't be effective at all

when is it possible to try to find first job?

You can try whenever you feel that you know enough to work on serious projects and basic tasks are already too simple for you
